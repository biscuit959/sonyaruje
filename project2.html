Mind Matterz

App Description: Mind Matterz is a platform designed to empower teens aged 13-16 by turning knowledge into an engaging game. Players dive into real-life scenarios using cards and QR codes, linking to an app that enriches the experience with interactive solutions. A unique blend of an interactive card game and app which encourages teens to think critically through gameplay and learn how to navigate relationships. The app aims to provide a fun and educational way for teens to learn about relationships, decision-making, and real-life scenarios through interactive gameplay.

App Features

Key Features:

  • Interactive card game mechanics
  • QR code integration for real-world scenarios
  • Customizable avatars and profiles
  • Progress tracking and rewards system
  • Educational content tailored to teen interests

    • A mix of tech and tradition! 3+ players can come together and chat about dilemmas. Find out what your friends and family really think! There are three different packs of cards that tackle the separate categories of life problems. Each card has a problem on it waiting to be discussed and solved. scan the QR code and on the app you can see the options.

    Design and Development

    Mind Matterz was developed using a combination of Figma for design and a robust tech stack for implementation. The design process involved creating user personas, wireframes, and prototypes to ensure a user-centered approach. The development team utilized agile methodologies to iterate on feedback and enhance the app's features continuously.
